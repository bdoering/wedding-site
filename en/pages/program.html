Program

Summary: The ceremony will take place on the 23rd of August 2014 at 12pm at the cityhall of Puchheim. The party will start from 3:30pm on close to Eurasburg (a 45 min drive from Puchheim).

For those of you who plan to arrive on Friday, we would like to all meet up in a Beer garden in the late afternoon.

And if you’re staying overnight for the party, we would like to invite you to a farewell-brunch on Sunday morning.

Where is what? Below is an overview:

Beer garden (late Friday afternoon)

You will already be around on Friday and you want to directly dive into Bavarian culture? Then join us at a beer garden! We will take care of the picnic and you can order your drinks from the beer garden.

Where indeed? We haven’t decided that yet but it will be somewhere in the West of Munich (reachable by public transportation in any case). We will come back to you with a location at the beginning of August at the latest (and an alternative solution in case of rain).

Civil ceremony in Puchheim (Saturday, 12pm)

   Poststraße 2
   82178 Puchheim

The ceremony will take place at the city hall of Puchheim, a city close to Germering where it is actually possible to get married on a Saturday! The city hall is located close to the Puchheim S-Bahn station on the line S4 (MVV suburban train system) and can be easily reached by public transportation from Munich.

We will be there at 11:30 (depending on Perrine:-). The ceremony will start at 12, don’t be late, we are in Germany!

We will have a cocktail reception afterwards.

Oh, by the way: There is no dress code, we are in the countryside!

Party at the Landgasthof Berg (Saturday, from 3:30pm on)

   Schmiedberg 2
   82547 Berg bei Eurasburg

The guest house is most easily reached by car. Therefore, we will try to organize some car-pooling for those of you who will not have any car. In case there is not enough place, we will organize some shuttle from the S-Bahn station Wolfratshausen (S7) (MVV) to the guest house.

The party will take place in the garden and the reception room of the Landgasthof Berg. After a “Kaffee und Kuchen” (German afternoon snack made of coffee and cake) in the afternoon, we will have dinner there together and party into the night!

By the way: the guest house can also provide accommodation!

Brunch at the Landgasthof Berg (Sunday morning)

If you stay around overnight and do not oversleep, we would like to invite you to join us for a Bavarian farewell-brunch (Weißwurstfrühstück) in order to gather strength before leaving!

Accommodation

Are you staying for the night from Saturday to Sunday? Are you possibly also looking for some accommodation for Friday night?

This is the situation: The party is at the Landgasthof Berg, where we have reserved all available rooms. Unfortunately, the number of beds is not sufficient to accommodate everybody. Therefore, we have also pre-reserved some rooms at the nearby Sprengenöder Alm. For the more adventurous, there are also indoor camping and outdoor camping possibilities.

Besides these options, there are of course other hotels/hostels available in the neighborhood. In this case, please organize them yourself.

Landgasthof Berg (Party Location)

   http://www.landgasthof-berg.de

   Schmiedberg 2
   82547 Berg bei Eurasburg

This is our party location. The guest house has 9 rooms (mostly double rooms, some larger ones), which are all reserved for us.

A double room costs about 90 € a night.

If you would like to stay here, please get in contact with us so that we can coordinate room allocations.

Sprengenöder Alm

   http://www.sprengenoeder-alm.de/

   Sprengenöd 4
   82547 Eurasburg

The Sprengenöder Alm is not even ten minutes away by car. We have pre-reserved several rooms there, which might provide a more quiet stay for Saturday night!

Double rooms cost 80 € per night, single rooms come at 50 € per night.

If you would like to stay there, please get in contact with us so that we can coordinate room allocations.

Indoor Camping

If you like company then we might have the right option for you! We have booked the two available large “family rooms” in the Landgasthof Berg for people who might be comfortable enough to sleep on the ground.

Should it be difficult for you to bring an air mattress then we can certainly help to organize one for you.

Outdoor Camping

And then there is the option for the outdoor lovers! The owners of the Landgasthof Berg have allowed us to put up tents in the garden if we want to.

Given the limited number of rooms available, we would appreciate if somebody would consider this option. You will get an extra treat for next morning’s brunch!

Interested? Let us know! If you need a tent, we might be able to organize one.

Please get back to us!

Life is complicated enough! You can help us simplify it by getting back to us with the following information before June 30. Thanks!

  • Number of people coming (are you bringing a companion?)

  • When can we expect you? Already on Friday evening, on Saturday at the city hall or later at the party? Also, would you like to have brunch with us on Sunday morning? See our program for details.

  • Do you want to stay for one or two nights in or near the country guest house (party location)? We have reserved some rooms, check our page on accommodation.

  • Are you coming by car and could you possibly take somebody along on Saturday?

  • Otherwise, do you need a means of transportation for Friday/Saturday?

Also, please check out our riddle game and possibly submit some nice pictures!
